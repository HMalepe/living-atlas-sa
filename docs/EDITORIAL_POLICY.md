# Editorial Policy — Living Atlas SA

## Mission

Publish trustworthy, layered knowledge about South African places, infrastructure, and skies — without flattening complexity or inventing certainty.

## What we publish

- Verifiable facts with attached sources
- Well-sourced historical interpretation (labelled as such)
- Community memory and oral history (labelled as such)
- Explicit unknowns: "Research has not yet established this."
- Disputed claims shown alongside competing evidence

## What we do not publish

- AI-generated historical statements as verified fact
- Invented engineers, dates, costs, quotations, or community views
- Oral history presented as official documentation
- Single-narrative erasure of competing cultural interpretations
- Development sample data in production as historical truth

## Review workflow

```
draft → in_review → published | disputed | archived | rejected
```

Community submissions add:

```
submitted → safety check → consent review → geographic review
         → editorial review → community-context review → published | disputed | rejected
```

## Roles and publishing authority

| Role | Can publish |
|------|-------------|
| viewer | No |
| contributor | Submit drafts |
| researcher | Create entities, submit for review |
| editor | Publish with source requirements met |
| moderator | Community content decisions |
| administrator | Full access |

## Source requirements

Every production claim requires at least one source reference OR explicit `unknown` confidence with editorial note.

## Cultural sensitivity

- Indigenous and community knowledge may require permission, attribution limits, or display restrictions
- Data model supports `display_restrictions`, `commercial_reuse`, `community_owner_org_id`
- AI may not determine cultural ownership or resolve disputes

## Corrections

- Corrections create new claim versions; history preserved in `change_history`
- Public correction notice for material errors

## Sponsorship

Sponsors may fund collections or research. Sponsors may not alter historical conclusions or suppress verified information.
